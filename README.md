# British Airways Review Analysis: A Tableau Analytics Project

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of **British Airways (BA) customer reviews**, utilizing data from 2017 to 2023. The objective is to identify key trends in passenger satisfaction across various service dimensions, aircraft types, and traveler categories. The insights are visualized through a **live interactive Tableau dashboard**, providing a data-driven perspective on BA's service quality.

## Key Insights & Findings
The analysis reveals several critical aspects of the passenger experience:
- **Overall Performance**: The airline maintains an **Average Overall Rating of 4.2**, though specific service areas show significant room for improvement.
- **Service Dimensions**: While **Cabin Staff Service (3.3)** and **Ground Service (3.0)** are rated moderately, **Seat Comfort (2.9)** and **Value for Money** are the lowest-rated categories, indicating a need for cabin interior upgrades and pricing strategy reviews.
- **Aircraft Performance**: Ratings vary significantly by aircraft. Modern wide-body aircraft like the **Boeing 787** and **Boeing 777** tend to receive higher satisfaction scores compared to older or narrow-body models.
- **Temporal Trends**: Passenger satisfaction has shown notable fluctuations over the years (2017-2023), likely influenced by global travel conditions and internal service changes.

### Dashboard Features:
- **Trend Analysis**: Monthly average ratings to track service quality over time.
- **Geographic Insights**: A global map visualization of review distributions and ratings.
- **Aircraft Comparison**: Performance benchmarking across different fleet models.
- **Multi-Dimensional Filters**: Ability to slice data by Traveler Type, Seat Type, and Recommendation status.
